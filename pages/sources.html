Data SourcesSumber Data数据来源தரவு மூலங்கள்سومبر داتا

All findings derive from 60 fieldwork photographs taken across 4 districts of Kuala Lumpur in April–May 2026, annotated in a master spreadsheet with 13 fields per sign.

Sampling Method

Each fieldworker collected 15 signs from their assigned district using a random-walk protocol: walk a designated transect, photograph the first sign at each intersection that meets the inclusion criteria. Inclusion criteria: (a) visible from a public sidewalk, (b) text-bearing, (c) not part of a private indoor space.

This yielded 4 districts × 15 signs = 60 total samples. Sample size is intentionally small and exploratory; findings about presence/absence patterns (ZH–TA non-coexistence, KB monolingual character) are stable, while findings about frequency ratios are sample-limited.
